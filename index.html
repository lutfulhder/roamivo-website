About Roamivo

Travel planning without endless searching

Planning a trip often requires checking many websites for attractions, transportation, accommodation areas, opening hours, family-friendly activities and budget options.

Roamivo aims to bring this information together and turn it into a clear, personalized, day-by-day travel plan.

Planned features

Built for practical trip planning

Personalized itineraries

Generate daily travel plans based on destination, dates, interests and preferred travel pace.

Budget-aware recommendations

Receive suggestions that consider the traveler's approximate budget and priorities.

Family-friendly planning

Discover activities and schedules suited to families travelling with children.

Local transportation guidance

Organize attractions and activities with practical local travel information.

Saved travel plans

Save, review and update personalized itineraries as travel plans change.

AI travel assistant

Ask travel-planning questions and receive recommendations based on the trip context.

Product status

Currently under active development

Roamivo is an early-stage, self-funded product. An initial MVP is being designed and developed to help travelers create faster, simpler and more personalized travel plans.

The planned cloud architecture may use AWS for backend APIs, secure data storage, monitoring, authentication and AI-powered itinerary generation.
