Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Roamivo is an early-stage travel-planning product currently under development. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you visit the Roamivo website or contact us.

If you contact Roamivo by email, we may receive information such as your name, email address and the content of your message.

How information is used

Information submitted by email may be used to:

  • Discuss potential early access

Information sharing

Roamivo does not sell personal information. Information may only be shared when necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations or protect legitimate rights.

Website infrastructure

The website may use infrastructure and security services provided by third-party technology providers. These providers may process limited technical information, such as IP addresses, browser information and security logs, according to their own privacy terms.

Cookies

The current website does not intentionally use advertising or marketing cookies. This policy may be updated if analytics, authentication or other product features are added.

Data retention

Email enquiries may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain business records or continue relevant communication.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights concerning access, correction or deletion of your personal information. You may contact us to make a request.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Roamivo develops and introduces new features. The latest version will be published on this page.
